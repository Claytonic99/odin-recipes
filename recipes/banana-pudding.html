Banana Pudding

Description

Pudding made with bananas

Ingredients (per bowl)

  • 1 Banana
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup butter
  • sugar
  • salt

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and slice it in half.
  2. Slice four circles of the banana off from the center and put aside for later.
  3. Smash the rest of the banana into a paste.
  4. Bring the milk to a boil.
  5. Mix the sugar and salt into the milk until thick.
  6. Remove the micture from the heat and stir in the banana paste and butter.
  7. Let sit in the fridge for 1 hour.
  8. Remove from fridge, garnish with banana slices and serve.
