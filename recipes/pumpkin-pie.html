Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pie made from a pumpkin.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pumpkin
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup butter

Steps

  1. Cut the pumpkin open and scoop out all the seeds.
  2. Smash the rest of the pumpkin into a paste.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  4. Mix the eggs and butter in a bowl with the pumpkin paste.
  5. Fill a pie tin with the mixture and bake in the oven for 30 minutes.
  6. Wash the pumpkin seeds and palce on a baking sheet.
  7. Remove the pie from oven and put the seeds in the oven with the baking sheet. Let the seeds bake for 10 minutes.
  8. Remove the seeds from the oven and use as a garnish for the pie.
