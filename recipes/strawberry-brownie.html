Strawberry Brownie

Description

Brownies made with strawberrie chuncks.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate brownie mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup butter
  • 4 cups strawberries
  • chocolate frosting

Steps

  1. Start preheating the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Chop 3 of the 4 cups of strawberries into small chunks.
  3. Open package of brownie mix into a mixing bowl with the eggs,butter, and chopped strawberry chunks.
  4. Bake in oven for 15 minutes.
  5. Spread chocolate frosting on top of the cake.
  6. Chop remanining cup of strawberries into halves.
  7. Garnish the cake with chopped strawberry halves.
  8. Cut the cake into brownie squares and serve.
